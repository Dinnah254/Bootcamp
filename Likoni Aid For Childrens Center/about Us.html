Who We Are

Likoni Aid For Children Center is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing support and resources to children in need. Our mission is to create a safe and nurturing environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive.

The organization was founded in the year 2000 by Rev. Ernest Ombeva Anzani in collaboration with the Timbwani community, after witnessing vulnerable children struggling in the slums of Timbwani.

What We Do

We are a charitable organization that cares for and protects vulnerable children through essential programs that ensure comfort, stability, successful transition, and a brighter future.

Our programs include:

  1. Basic Needs Support Sponsorship Program (feeding, clothing, accommodation, and security)
  2. Educational Sponsorship Program to ensure children stay in school and receive quality education
  3. Medical Care Program that caters to children's health needs
  4. Guidance and Counselling Program
  5. Vocational Training Program (tailoring, beadwork, T-shirt printing, and more)
  6. Volunteering Program for individuals who wish to support through skills, time, or resources

These programs are made possible through the generous support of well-wishers and volunteers. We warmly welcome volunteers to experience life at the orphanage, share skills, and contribute to improving the lives of children and caregivers.

Our Vision

"Raising hope for vulnerable African children through improved livelihoods and self-sustainability."

Our Mission

Our mission is to alleviate human suffering through child care and protection, advocacy, and community development, promoting self-sustainability and improved livelihoods for children, youth, and women.

"Our solid principles are rooted in faith, believing that each day we rise to fulfill our God-given responsibility to serve humanity. The lives of these children inspire us to keep going.

We are proud of the positive transformations and life-changing stories witnessed throughout our journey. Our love is priceless and given freely."

- Rev. Ernest Ombeva Anzani, Project Director
