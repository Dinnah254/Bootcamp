Our Programs

Our Current Sustainability Development Goals

  1. Promotion of quality education and zero tolerance to child abuse
  2. Provision of good nutrition, improved health services, and referrals
  3. Socio-economic empowerment through sports, talents, and vocational skills
  4. Charitable volunteer programs for sustainability and skills development
  5. Promotion of self-sustainability through community-based permaculture
  6. Sanitary towel program supporting vulnerable girls with hygiene packs

Our Challenges

In serving vulnerable children, we strive to provide the best care possible. However, limited manpower and financial resources have hindered effective service delivery.

Below are some of the key challenges where we humbly seek support and partnership:

  1. Lack of a permanent home for safe and stable accommodation. Frequent relocation affects children's psychological well-being.
  2. Limited funding for basic needs and education sponsorship programs, affecting school continuity.
  3. Need for partnerships to establish a sustainable international sponsorship program.
  4. Poor infrastructure and limited access to social amenities.
  5. Lack of clean and reliable water supply.
  6. Insufficient manpower and long-term sustainability support.
